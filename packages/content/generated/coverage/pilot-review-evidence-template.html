  1. Complete one evidence record per pack before promoting any reviewed variants to child runtime.

  3. Produced audio must be technically valid and human-listened for clarity, pace, warmth and pronunciation before approval.
  4. If a required lane is not approved, keep the pack in review and use revise or hold as the decision.
