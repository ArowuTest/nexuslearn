Teaching Journey

warm_up

ten-tens-exchange

Bundle ten tens. What new unit have you made, and what value stayed the same?

Reactivate the base-ten exchange that links two-digit understanding to hundreds.

Audio: Ten tens have the same value as one hundred. The arrangement changes, but the total does not.

Visual: Ten ten-rods sit beside one hundred-square with an equality link.

concept_launch

supplies-group-by-place

Build 374 crates using hundreds, tens and ones, then match the build to the numeral.

Introduce three-digit numerals as records of grouped quantities.

Audio: Three hundreds, seven tens and four ones make three hundred and seventy-four.

Visual: Explorer supply crates group into hundred pallets, ten-crate rows and single crates beside an HTO chart.

worked_example

empty-tens-placeholder

Build 406. There are four hundreds, no tens and six ones, so the zero must remain in the tens place.

Model zero as a placeholder and connect empty quantity columns to written notation.

Audio: The tens column is empty, but the zero keeps the hundreds and ones in their correct places.

Visual: Four hundred-squares and six ones align with 4, 0 and 6 in a place-value chart.

guided_practice

partition-links-lock

Build the number, write its expanded form, then show one different valid partition.

Coordinate concrete, pictorial and symbolic representations while introducing flexible partitioning.

Audio: First use standard parts. Then exchange one hundred or one ten and check that the total is unchanged.

Visual: A base-ten workspace links to numeral, words, expanded notation and a total counter.

checkpoint

left-to-right-compare

Which is greater, 509 or 590? Then find 10 less than the greater number.

Detect digit-by-digit comparison errors and insecure changes across tens.

Audio: The hundreds match, so compare the tens. To find ten less, change the tens value while tracking any exchange.

Visual: Two HTO charts are inspected left to right, then the selected number moves one ten-step on a number line.

teach_back

boundary-change-explain

Teach your explorer partner why 398 becomes 408 when 10 is added.

Gather explanation evidence for exchange across a hundred boundary.

Audio: Show what happens to the tens and hundreds, and explain why the ones stay the same.

Visual: Base-ten blocks, an HTO chart and a marked number line remain visible for the learner's explanation.

ma-y3-place-value-to-1000-q-build-374

base-ten-build review difficulty 3

Build 374 using hundreds, tens and ones.

374 is 3 hundreds, 7 tens and 4 ones, which is 300 + 70 + 4.

ma-y3-place-value-to-1000-q-placeholder-406

place-value-chart review difficulty 4

Which expanded form matches 406?

406 has 4 hundreds, 0 tens and 6 ones, so its value is 400 + 6.

ma-y3-place-value-to-1000-q-compare-509-590

number-line-compare review difficulty 5

Choose the greater number and explain which place decides it.

The hundreds are equal, so compare the tens. Nine tens is greater than zero tens, so 590 is greater than 509.

ma-y3-place-value-to-1000-q-ten-more-398

change-by-10-100 review difficulty 7

Find 10 more than 398 and choose the places that change.

398 plus 10 is 408. The 9 tens become 10 tens, which exchange for 1 hundred, so the hundreds and tens digits change.

Adaptive Support

low_sensory

Use a plain HTO chart, muted island-map background, one highlighted place at a time and no particle celebrations.

reduced_motion

Replace moving base-ten exchanges and number-line jumps with labelled before/after panels controlled by a Next button.

audio_first

Read every number in full, then announce digit, place and value; provide separate replay for the prompt and each representation.

reading_support

Keep instructions to one action per sentence, pair words with H/T/O labels and leave quantity models visible while symbols are interpreted.

attention_support

Use a consistent build-read-partition-check routine, hide inactive controls and show a short progress strip.

processing_support

Remove timers, allow unlimited exchanges and undo, and offer side-by-side rather than transient comparisons.

confidence_support

Preserve correctly built places during repair and describe a wrong answer as a place-value clue to inspect.

eal_support

Preteach hundred, ten, one, digit, value, greater, less and equal with matched blocks, gestures and spoken examples.
