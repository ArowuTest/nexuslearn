Teaching Journey

warm_up

idea-card-match

Which two sentences are about the same idea?

Check related-idea recognition before paragraph building.

Audio: A paragraph groups sentences that belong together.

Visual: Sentence cards glow when they share an idea.

concept_launch

notebook-bundle-open

Sort the explorer notes into paragraph bundles.

Introduce paragraph grouping through visible clustering.

Audio: Put notes about the same thing into one paragraph bundle.

Visual: Explorer notebook pages with draggable sentence strips.

worked_example

theme-ribbon-tie

These three sentences all describe the cave entrance.

Model theme label and related sentence grouping.

Audio: Because all three sentences describe the entrance, they belong in one paragraph.

Visual: Three cave-entrance sentences tie together under one label.

guided_practice

sentence-strip-snap

Drag each sentence into the best paragraph group.

Practise grouping by meaning, not surface features.

Audio: Read or listen for what the sentence is mostly about.

Visual: Two paragraph zones labelled setting and action.

checkpoint

odd-sentence-pulse

Which sentence does not belong in this paragraph?

Detect unrelated-idea mixing.

Audio: One sentence changes the idea. Find the odd one out.

Visual: A paragraph bundle shakes when an unrelated sentence is included.

teach_back

companion-paragraph-repair

Teach Isla why we do not start a new paragraph after every sentence.

Repair paragraph-after-every-sentence misconception.

Audio: I made every sentence its own paragraph. Why does that feel jumpy?

Visual: The companion compares scattered cards with a grouped paragraph.

Question Variants

en-y3-writing-paragraph-grouping-q-same-idea

sentence-sort approved difficulty 4

Which sentence belongs with 'The cave entrance was narrow and dark'?

Both sentences describe the cave entrance, so they belong together.

en-y3-writing-paragraph-grouping-q-theme-label

theme-choice approved difficulty 4

Choose the best theme label: 'The river twisted through the trees. Moss covered the rocks. Birds called overhead.'

The sentences all describe the forest setting.

en-y3-writing-paragraph-grouping-q-odd-sentence

paragraph-build approved difficulty 5

Which sentence does not belong in a paragraph about the storm?

'The cake tasted sweet' does not belong because it is not about the storm.
