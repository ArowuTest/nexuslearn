Teaching Journey

warm_up

testable-question-sort

Which question could be tested with measurements?

Separate scientific testable questions from opinions or impossible questions.

Audio: A testable question can be investigated using observations or measurements.

Visual: Question cards sort into testable, opinion and too broad.

concept_launch

investigation-planner-columns

Use the investigation planner to choose what to change, measure and keep the same.

Introduce variable roles as the backbone of fair investigation planning.

Audio: Change one thing, measure one result and keep important conditions the same.

Visual: Three planner columns labelled change, measure and keep the same.

worked_example

paper-towel-planner-worked

Plan a fair test for which paper towel absorbs the most water.

Model variable identification, safe method order and measurement choice.

Audio: We change the paper towel type, measure the volume of water absorbed and keep the water volume and towel size the same.

Visual: Planner fills with variable cards and a simple measurement table.

guided_practice

poe-evidence-cycle

Predict, observe the trial data, then explain whether the prediction was supported.

Connect predictions and conclusions to evidence rather than correctness or preference.

Audio: A prediction can be supported or not supported by evidence. Either result can teach us something.

Visual: Prediction, observation and explanation cards link to a data table.

checkpoint

one-change-many-locks

In a fair test, should the independent variable stay the same?

Repair the misconception that fair means every variable stays unchanged.

Audio: In a fair test, the independent variable changes on purpose. Control variables stay the same.

Visual: One change card unlocks while control cards stay locked.

teach_back

companion-conclusion-repair

Teach Mira how to improve a conclusion so it uses evidence.

Consolidate evidence-linked conclusion writing.

Audio: I wrote what I thought would happen. Can you help me use the measurements?

Visual: Companion conclusion is revised by dragging evidence from a table into a sentence frame.

Question Variants

sc-y7-scientific-method-q-paper-towel-variables

variable-sort review difficulty 5

A pupil tests which paper towel absorbs the most water. Sort the variables.

The paper towel type is changed, the water absorbed is measured, and towel size plus starting water volume are kept the same.

sc-y7-scientific-method-q-fair-test

investigation-planner review difficulty 6

Plan a fair test for how ramp height affects the distance a toy car travels.

A fair plan changes ramp height, measures distance travelled, keeps other important conditions the same and repeats measurements.

sc-y7-scientific-method-q-poe-seeds

prediction-observation-explanation review difficulty 7

Predict whether seeds with more water will germinate faster, observe the data, then choose the best explanation.

The evidence suggests that more water is not always better. A good explanation responds to the data rather than defending the original prediction.

sc-y7-scientific-method-q-conclusion-evidence

conclusion-evidence-match review difficulty 6

A class tests three materials for insulation. Which conclusion uses evidence best?

The foam cover is supported by the evidence because it had the smallest temperature decrease, so it reduced heat transfer most in this test.

sc-y7-scientific-method-q-testable-question

variable-sort review difficulty 4

Which investigation question is most testable?

The light question is testable because the amount of light can be changed and seedling height can be measured over a set time.

Adaptive Support

low_sensory

Use a quiet planner workspace with no timers, flashing or competitive scoring.

reduced_motion

Reveal planner cards and data tables instantly or step-by-step without animated movement.

audio_first

Read investigation questions, variable cards and data rows aloud with replay controls.

reading_support

Use sentence frames, icons for change-measure-keep, glossary chips and short method strips.

attention_support

Show one planner decision at a time and park extra cards in a collapsed tray.

processing_support

Allow unlimited planning time, undo, printable summaries and side-by-side prediction/data/conclusion views.

confidence_support

Normalise unsupported predictions as useful evidence and separate planning feedback from correctness judgement.

eal_support

Preteach variable, change, measure, control, prediction, observation, evidence, conclusion, reliable and repeat.
