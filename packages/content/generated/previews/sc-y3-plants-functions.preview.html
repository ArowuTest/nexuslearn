Teaching Journey

warm_up

plant-need-toggle

What does this plant need to grow well?

Reactivate basic plant needs before connecting parts to functions.

Audio: Plants need things like water and light. Their parts help them get what they need.

Visual: A small plant changes when water or light icons are added.

concept_launch

greenhouse-part-scan

Open the greenhouse scanner to see each plant part doing a job.

Introduce the idea that plant parts have functions.

Audio: Every part of a plant has a job.

Visual: A scanner highlights roots, stem, leaves and flower in sequence.

worked_example

water-up-stem

Watch water move from roots up the stem.

Model root and stem functions as connected systems.

Audio: The roots take in water. The stem carries it upwards.

Visual: Blue water dots move from soil through roots and up the stem.

guided_practice

function-card-snap

Drag each job card to the plant part that does it.

Link vocabulary, visual part and function.

Audio: Read or listen to the job, then match it to the plant part.

Visual: A labelled plant with draggable function cards.

checkpoint

root-block-test

What happens if the roots cannot take in water?

Check causal reasoning rather than memorised labels.

Audio: Predict what will happen, then run the greenhouse test.

Visual: A simulation blocks root water intake and shows the plant wilting.

teach_back

companion-leaf-question

Teach Sol why leaves are not just decoration.

Use explanation to repair leaf-function misconceptions.

Audio: I thought leaves were just pretty. Can you show me their job?

Visual: The companion points at leaves while sunlight appears.

Adaptive Support

low_sensory

Use calm greenhouse colours and avoid wilting effects that feel punitive.

reduced_motion

Offer static scan layers and before-after panels instead of flowing water animations.

audio_first

Narrate each part name and function with replayable vocabulary cards.

reading_support

Pair each function sentence with an icon and spoken explanation.

attention_support

One test variable changes at a time in the simulation.

processing_support

Allow learners to pause the scanner and inspect labels before answering.

confidence_support

Use 'scientist test' language so wrong predictions become experiments.

eal_support

Preteach root, stem, leaf, flower, water, light, seed and because with visuals.
