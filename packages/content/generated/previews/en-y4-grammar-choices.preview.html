Teaching Journey

warm_up

pronoun-thread-scan

Who does 'she' mean in this sentence pair?

Activate pronoun reference before editing for cohesion.

Audio: A clear pronoun points back to one person, place or thing.

Visual: Pronoun threads connect to possible noun cards, with unclear threads shown as tangled.

concept_launch

clarity-meter-choice

Choose whether the next sentence needs 'it', 'the machine' or 'the tiny motor'.

Introduce grammar choices as decisions that help the reader follow meaning.

Audio: Sometimes a pronoun is smooth. Sometimes a noun phrase is clearer.

Visual: A clarity meter compares three replacement cards in a short paragraph.

worked_example

ambiguous-pronoun-untangle

Maya gave Aisha the map because she trusted her. Who trusted whom?

Model spotting ambiguous pronouns and repairing them with a noun or noun phrase.

Audio: If a pronoun could point to more than one person, use a name or noun phrase to make it clear.

Visual: Two character cards both tug on 'she' until one is replaced with a name.

guided_practice

relationship-bridge-lock

Choose the bridge word that shows why the team stopped.

Practise choosing conjunctions, adverbs or prepositions for time and cause relationships.

Audio: Ask whether the link shows time, cause, order or contrast before choosing it.

Visual: Sentence bridges are labelled time, cause and contrast; only the matching bridge locks.

checkpoint

repetition-clarity-balance

Which edit avoids repeating 'the dragon' but keeps the meaning clear?

Detect over-repetition and unclear replacement errors.

Audio: Good cohesion is not just fewer repeated words. It must still be clear.

Visual: A repetition alarm quietens only when the replacement still points to the right noun.

teach_back

companion-cohesion-repair

Rex changed every noun to 'it'. Teach Rex when to keep a noun phrase.

Use explanation to secure purposeful noun and pronoun choices.

Audio: I made it shorter, but now I cannot tell what 'it' means. Can you help me choose?

Visual: The companion's paragraph becomes tangled, then clears as noun phrases return where needed.

Question Variants

en-y4-grammar-choices-q-pronoun-clear-otter

pronoun-cohesion-choice review difficulty 3

In this sentence, what does 'it' mean? The otter found a shell and carried it to the riverbank.

'It' means the shell because the shell is the thing being carried.

en-y4-grammar-choices-q-avoid-dragon-repeat

noun-pronoun-repair review difficulty 4

Choose the clearest edit: The dragon opened the gate. The dragon stepped into the cave.

'It' avoids repeating 'the dragon' and is clear because only the dragon can step into the cave.

en-y4-grammar-choices-q-ambiguous-she

cohesion-edit review difficulty 6

Which edit makes the meaning clearest? Maya thanked Aisha because she had found the missing compass.

Repeating 'Aisha' is clearer here because it shows that Aisha found the compass.

en-y4-grammar-choices-q-cause-link

time-cause-link-choice review difficulty 4

Choose the best link: The path was flooded, ___ the hikers chose a safer route.

'So' shows that the flooded path caused the hikers to choose a safer route.

en-y4-grammar-choices-q-time-preposition

time-cause-link-choice review difficulty 5

Choose the best time phrase: ___ the science lesson, we tested which material was waterproof.

'During the science lesson' tells when the testing happened.

Adaptive Support

low_sensory

Use calm editing-workbench colours, no flashing error marks and optional plain-text mode.

reduced_motion

Use static pronoun threads and before-and-after edit cards instead of animated tangles.

audio_first

Read sentence pairs aloud with names and pronouns lightly emphasised; allow replay before choosing.

reading_support

Chunk short paragraphs into sentence cards with line focus, glossary popovers and dyslexia-friendly spacing.

attention_support

Ask one decision at a time: who or what is meant, whether repetition helps or hurts, then which link fits.

processing_support

Allow hide-and-compare choices, undo, oral rehearsal and explanation frames such as 'This pronoun means...'.

confidence_support

Frame edits as reader-help choices and reward clear explanations even when the first choice needs repair.

eal_support

Preteach pronoun, noun phrase, link, cause, time, clear and repeat with icons and paired oral examples.
