Turn on the ray model and watch light travel in straight lines.

Introduce ray models as explanatory diagrams.

shadow-zone-build

The opaque block stops some rays, so a shadow forms behind it.

Model cause of shadow formation.

Audio: Where the object blocks the light, the screen stays dark.

Visual: Straight rays stop at block edges and create a shadow zone.

shadow-size-slider

Move the object closer to the torch and predict the shadow.

shadow-outline-project

Check straight-line reasoning for shape.

Audio: Light travels in straight lines, so the blocked area follows the object's outline.

Visual: Object outline projects to shadow outline.
