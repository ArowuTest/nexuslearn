Teaching Journey

warm_up

whole-size-calibrator

Check: are these fraction bars the same-size whole?

Secure fair comparison before equivalent fraction reasoning.

Audio: Before we compare fractions, the wholes must be the same size.

Visual: Two fraction bars resize until their whole lengths match.

concept_launch

fraction-bar-align

Slide the fraction bars until the shaded endpoints match.

Introduce equivalent fractions as equal amounts with different partitions.

Audio: One half and two quarters reach the same point, so they are equivalent.

Visual: Stacked bars for 1/2, 2/4 and 4/8 align with a glowing endpoint.

worked_example

numerator-counter-snap

Three eighths plus two eighths makes five eighths.

Model adding numerators while the same denominator names the equal parts.

Audio: The parts are still eighths, so we count three eighths and two more eighths.

Visual: Eighths tiles snap into one bar while the denominator stays fixed.

guided_practice

hundred-square-fill

Colour 37 hundredths on the hundred-square.

Connect counting in hundredths to a 10 by 10 area model.

Audio: Fill three full rows and seven more squares to show thirty-seven hundredths.

Visual: A hundred-square fills by tens then ones.

checkpoint

denominator-piece-compare

Which is larger: one third or one sixth?

Detect larger-denominator-means-larger misconception.

Audio: If one whole is split into more equal parts, each part is smaller.

Visual: A cake split into thirds and sixths, then one piece from each is compared.

teach_back

companion-denominator-lock

Rex says 2/5 + 1/5 = 3/10. Teach Rex what stayed the same.

Use explanation to repair adding-denominators errors.

Audio: I added the bottom numbers too. Can you show me why the pieces are still fifths?

Visual: The companion changes fifths into tenths, then the learner locks the denominator.

Question Variants

ma-y4-fractions-q-half-equivalent

fraction-bar-match review difficulty 4

Which fraction is equivalent to 1/2?

2/4 is equivalent to 1/2 because both show half of the same-size whole.

ma-y4-fractions-q-add-fifths

same-denominator-build review difficulty 5

Find 2/5 + 1/5.

The denominator stays 5 because the parts are still fifths. 2 fifths plus 1 fifth is 3 fifths.

ma-y4-fractions-q-third-sixth-compare

tap-choice review difficulty 4

Which fraction is larger when the wholes are the same size?

1/3 is larger because splitting a whole into 3 equal parts makes bigger pieces than splitting it into 6 equal parts.

ma-y4-fractions-q-hundredths-37

hundred-square-count review difficulty 5

A hundred-square has 37 small squares shaded. What fraction is shaded?

37 out of 100 equal parts is 37/100.

ma-y4-fractions-q-subtract-eighths

same-denominator-build review difficulty 6

Find 7/8 - 3/8.

Subtract the numerators and keep the denominator: 7/8 - 3/8 = 4/8.

Adaptive Support

low_sensory

Use calm fraction bars with no flashing slice effects.

reduced_motion

Use static aligned bars and instant tile add/remove states.

audio_first

Read each fraction as 'numerator over denominator' and as counted parts, such as 'three fifths'.

reading_support

Use labelled numerator, denominator, whole, equal parts and hundredths cards.

attention_support

Present one decision at a time: same whole, equal parts, then answer.

processing_support

confidence_support
