Teaching Journey

warm_up

term-trays-light

Sort numbers, x terms and x squared terms into different trays.

Activate the idea that term families must match before they can combine.

Audio: Terms can only join if their variable part matches.

Visual: Three lab trays labelled number, x and x squared.

concept_launch

algebra-sorter-start

The lab sorter only combines tokens from the same family.

Introduce like terms as same-family algebra objects.

Audio: Like terms are from the same family. x terms join x terms.

Visual: Tokens snap only into matching trays.

worked_example

x-tiles-combine

3x + 5x - 2 becomes 8x - 2. The x terms join; the number stays separate.

Model collection while preserving unlike number terms.

Audio: Three x plus five x is eight x. The minus two is a number term, so it stays separate.

Visual: 3 x tiles join 5 x tiles; the -2 token remains in the number tray.

guided_practice

sign-tab-lock

Drag each term with its sign into the right tray.

Make the sign travel with the term.

Audio: The sign belongs to the term. Move them together.

Visual: Negative signs are attached to tiles as tabs.

checkpoint

unlike-terms-bounce

Can 4x and 3x squared be combined?

Detect whether the learner combines unlike terms.

Audio: Check the variable part before you combine.

Visual: x and x squared tiles reject a merge attempt.

teach_back

assistant-wrong-merge

Teach the lab assistant why 2x + 3 cannot become 5x.

Use explanation to confirm the boundary between variables and constants.

Audio: Can you explain why a number term cannot join an x term?

Visual: The assistant tries a wrong merge and waits for correction.

Question Variants

ma-y7-algebra-simplify-expressions-q-like-terms

multiple_choice approved difficulty 4

Simplify 3x + 5x - 2.

3x and 5x are like terms, so they combine to make 8x. The -2 remains separate.

ma-y7-algebra-simplify-expressions-q-negative-sign

symbol-build review difficulty 5

Simplify 7x - 2x + 4.

7x minus 2x leaves 5x, and the +4 remains because it is a number term.

ma-y7-algebra-simplify-expressions-q-unlike-terms

short-response review difficulty 7

Explain why 4x + 3x^2 cannot be simplified to 7x^2.

4x and 3x^2 are unlike terms. Their variable parts are different, so they cannot be collected together.
