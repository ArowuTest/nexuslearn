Teaching Journey

warm_up

equal-parts-scan

Which shape is split into equal parts?

Check equal-part understanding before naming tenths.

Audio: Fractions need equal parts.

Visual: Two explorer tiles compare equal and unequal cuts.

concept_launch

tenths-bridge-build

The island bridge has ten equal planks. Each plank is one tenth.

Introduce tenths as one whole split into ten equal parts.

Audio: Ten equal planks make one whole bridge. One plank is one tenth.

Visual: A bridge appears with ten equal planks across a river.

worked_example

tenths-planks-glow

Three shaded planks out of ten equal planks is three tenths.

Model numerator and denominator meaning with a concrete representation.

Audio: The ten tells us the whole has ten equal parts. The three tells us three are shaded.

Visual: Three planks glow while the denominator label stays under all ten.

guided_practice

fraction-wall-fill

Shade four of the ten equal parts.

Let the learner construct tenths before symbolic selection.

Audio: Count the equal parts you shade, but keep all ten parts in the whole.

Visual: A fraction wall with ten tappable equal tiles.

checkpoint

tenths-number-line-step

Where does one tenth go between 0 and 1?

Detect whether the learner treats one tenth as less than one whole.

Audio: One tenth is one step out of ten between zero and one.

Visual: A number line from 0 to 1 split into ten equal jumps.

teach_back

companion-whole-repair

Teach Rafi why three tenths is not the same as three wholes.

Confirm denominator meaning through explanation.

Audio: Can you show me what the ten means?

Visual: The companion tries to make three wholes and waits for correction.

fraction-wall review difficulty 3

Shade three tenths of the bridge.

ma-y3-number-fractions-tenths-q-one-tenth-line

number-line review difficulty 4
