energy-story-builder

Build an energy story using stores and transfer arrows.

Introduce stores and pathways as a model with precise roles.

worked_example

torch-energy-transfer-sim

Follow the energy from the battery to the lamp and surroundings.

Model useful and dissipated transfers without implying energy disappears.

Audio: Energy is transferred electrically from the chemical store in the battery. Some is transferred by radiation as light and some by heating to the surroundings.

Visual: Colour-coded arrows move from battery chemical store to lamp and surroundings.

thermal-transfer-slider

checkpoint

store-pathway-lock

Is heating a store or a transfer pathway?

Detect confusion between stores and pathways.

Audio: Heating is a transfer pathway. Thermal energy store is the store.

Visual: Heating arrow is sorted away from thermal store card.

Visual: Companion points to a warm phone and surrounding air arrows.

Question Variants

sc-y7-energy-q-torch-story

energy-transfer-simulator review difficulty 5

Build the best energy story for a torch that is switched on.

Energy is transferred electrically from the chemical store in the battery. Some energy is transferred by radiation as light and some by heating to the surroundings.

sc-y7-energy-q-heating-pathway

store-pathway-sort review difficulty 4

Sort each card as an energy store or a transfer pathway.

Thermal, chemical and kinetic are stores. Heating and radiation are transfer pathways.

sc-y7-energy-q-phone-warm

explain-choice review difficulty 6

A phone gets warm while charging. Which explanation is most scientific?

Energy transferred to the phone and surroundings by heating is conserved but becomes spread out and less useful.

sc-y7-energy-q-insulation-poe

prediction-observation-explanation review difficulty 7

Predict what happens when the cup is given thicker insulation, observe the simulator, then choose the best explanation.

Thicker insulation reduces the rate of energy transfer by heating to the surroundings, so the cup's thermal store decreases more slowly.

Adaptive Support

low_sensory

Use calm dark-lab colours, no flashing energy effects and a single highlighted pathway at a time.

reduced_motion

Replace moving arrows with numbered static snapshots and optional slow pulse animation.

audio_first

Narrate each store and transfer from left to right with replayable vocabulary cards.

reading_support

Use icon-supported store cards, short explanation stems and a persistent glossary for store, transfer and dissipated.

attention_support

Limit the active card bank to the current system and reveal dissipated transfers only after the useful pathway is placed.

processing_support

Allow freeze-frame inspection, step-by-step replay and side-by-side before/after simulator panels.

confidence_support

Frame wrong energy stories as models to improve using evidence from the simulator.

eal_support

Preteach store, transfer, pathway, surroundings, useful, dissipated, heating, radiation and electrical with paired icons.
