Teaching Journey

warm_up

inside-edge-toggle

Tap the inside space of the shape.

Separate area from perimeter before measuring.

Visual: Inside fill and outside edge toggle on a rectangle.

concept_launch

floor-tile-fill

Cover the floor with equal square tiles.

Introduce area as counting square units.

Audio: To measure area, cover the space with equal squares.

Visual: A workshop floor fills with square tiles.

worked_example

area-array-highlight

A 4 by 5 rectangle has 4 rows of 5 squares.

Connect counting squares to array multiplication.

Audio: Four rows of five square units makes twenty square units.

Visual: Rows and columns highlight on a 4 by 5 grid.

tile-snap-grid

Fill the rectangle and count the square units.

Practise complete tiling without gaps or overlaps.

Audio: Each square tile counts once.

Visual: Learner drags square tiles into a rectangular grid.

Audio: Area counts inside squares, not the outside edge.

Visual: Inside tiles and outside border compete as two answer paths.

companion-area-repair

Repair additive rectangle misconception.

Audio: I added the length and width. Why did I miss some squares?

Visual: The companion counts only one row and one column before repair.
