Teaching Journey

warm_up

signed-context-align

Place temperatures, floors and elevations on one vertical scale.

Reactivate negative numbers as values below a reference point rather than as a special set of labels.

Audio: Zero is a reference point. Values can extend in both directions from it.

Visual: Temperature, lift-floor and elevation cards align to a shared signed scale.

concept_launch

signed-line-scan

Compare signed numbers by locating them, not by looking only at their digits.

Establish that position determines order and that values increase to the right.

Audio: The number farther to the right is greater, even when both numbers are negative.

Visual: A horizontal number line scans from -10 through zero to 10.

worked_example

negative-order-compare

Why is -3 greater than -8 even though 8 is the larger digit?

Model comparison using position, distance and context while separating magnitude from order.

Audio: Negative three is closer to zero and lies to the right of negative eight, so negative three is greater.

Visual: Points at -8 and -3 appear with distance bars to zero and a greater-than arrow.

guided_practice

opposite-transform-move

Rewrite each subtraction as adding the opposite, then show the movement.

Connect subtraction of signed integers to a consistent number-line model.

Audio: Subtracting a number is equivalent to adding its opposite. Subtracting negative six means adding positive six.

Visual: The expression -4 - (-6) transforms into -4 + 6 before the arrow moves right.

checkpoint

signed-arrow-test

Which direction and distance solve 2 - (-5)?

Detect rote left-for-subtraction reasoning and check whether the learner reads the sign of the number being subtracted.

Audio: First change subtract negative five into add positive five, then move five places right.

Visual: Three candidate arrows start at 2 and show different directions and lengths.

teach_back

companion-sign-role-repair

Teach Lyra why 'two negatives make a positive' is not a complete explanation for every calculation.

Replace an overgeneralised slogan with operation-specific reasoning.

Audio: Which negative is the operation, which negative belongs to the number, and what equivalent addition does the expression show?

Visual: The companion labels the subtraction sign and the sign of the second integer separately.

Question Variants

ma-y7-negative-numbers-q-order

number-line review difficulty 4

Place -7, -2, 0 and 4 on the number line, then order them from least to greatest.

-7 is farthest left, followed by -2, then 0 and 4, so -7 < -2 < 0 < 4.

ma-y7-negative-numbers-q-temperature-change

operation-model review difficulty 5

At dawn the temperature is -3°C. By midday it is 5°C. Show the change and find how much the temperature increased.

The temperature rises 3°C to reach zero and another 5°C to reach 5°C, for an increase of 8°C.

ma-y7-negative-numbers-q-subtract-negative

operation-model review difficulty 6

Calculate -4 - (-6). First choose the equivalent addition, then show the movement on the number line.

-4 - (-6) is equivalent to -4 + 6. Moving six places right from -4 lands on 2.

ma-y7-negative-numbers-q-explain-comparison

reason-choice review difficulty 5

Which explanation correctly compares -8 and -3?

-3 is greater than -8 because -3 lies farther right and is closer to zero; the digit size alone does not determine the order of negative numbers.

Adaptive Support

low_sensory

Use a calm grid, steady arrows and no rapid scrolling, countdowns or alarm-style feedback.

reduced_motion

Offer static start-change-end frames and numbered hops instead of animated travel along the line.

audio_first

Read signed numbers with deliberate pauses, for example 'subtract negative six', and narrate start, direction and distance.

reading_support

Keep contexts short, show the expression beside the model and use consistent icons for start, change and end.

attention_support

Reveal one decision at a time: locate, choose equivalent operation, choose direction, calculate and check.

processing_support

Allow integer chips, a vertical scale or a written step table as alternatives to mental movement.

confidence_support

Preserve correct positioning or direction as partial evidence and route only the failed sign decision to repair.

eal_support

Preteach negative, positive, below, above, increase, decrease, opposite, difference and greater than with paired visual examples.
