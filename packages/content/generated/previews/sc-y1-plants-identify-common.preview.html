Teaching Journey

warm_up

plant-hotspot-glow

Tap the plant part you can see.

Encourage observation before naming.

Audio: Scientists look closely before they name things.

Visual: Large plant pictures with leaf, stem and flower hotspots.

concept_launch

garden-scanner-sweep

Open the garden scanner and find three kinds of plant.

Introduce plants as a varied group, not just flowers.

Audio: Some plants are flowers, some are trees, and some are grasses.

Visual: A child-safe garden scanner sweeps over plants.

worked_example

tree-card-compare

This pine is an evergreen tree because it stays green.

Model simple classification using visible features.

Audio: A pine tree keeps green needles, so it is evergreen.

Visual: Pine and oak comparison cards.

guided_practice

plant-card-basket-snap

Sort the pictures into wild plants, garden plants and trees.

Practise identifying broad plant groups.

Audio: Drag each picture to the group it belongs to.

Visual: Three baskets with picture cards.

checkpoint

season-tree-toggle

Which tree is evergreen?

Check evergreen/deciduous distinction.

Audio: Evergreen trees keep their green leaves or needles.

Visual: Two trees shown across seasons.

teach_back

companion-plant-family

Teach Pip why a tree is also a plant.

Repair over-narrow plant category misconceptions.

Audio: I thought plants were only flowers. Can you show me?

Visual: The companion compares flower, grass and tree cards.
