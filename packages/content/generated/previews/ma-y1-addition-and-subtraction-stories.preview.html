Teaching Journey

warm_up

small-group-calm-reveal

Look at the picture, then tap or build how many objects there are.

Activate careful counting, subitising and numeral matching before a story changes the set.

Audio: See the group first. Count each object once if you need to.

Visual: Up to ten large objects appear in familiar small-group and ten-frame arrangements.

concept_launch

story-panels-open

Show the story in three pictures: what we start with, what changes and what the result is.

Establish a consistent story structure before operation symbols are introduced.

Audio: First show the start. Then show what happens. Last show the amount we need.

Visual: Three large panels labelled START, CHANGE and RESULT with replayable narration.

worked_example

birds-arrive-count-on

There are six birds. Three more arrive. How many birds are there now?

Model joining with objects, count-on strategy, spoken equation and equality.

Audio: Keep six in your head and count on: seven, eight, nine. Six plus three equals nine.

Visual: Six birds remain grouped while three arrive one at a time; the equation appears only after the model.

guided_practice

whole-away-left

Build eleven cubes. Move four away. Count what stays and choose the matching equation.

Coordinate starting whole, removed part, remaining part and subtraction notation.

Audio: Start with the whole. Move four away once. The cubes still on the mat are left.

Visual: A whole tray, away tray and remaining tray preserve every cube.

checkpoint

rows-align-gap

One row has eight shells and another has five. How many more shells are in the first row?

Diagnose comparison as a difference situation rather than assuming every subtraction story means objects leave.

Audio: Match the rows. The extra unmatched shells show the difference.

Visual: Two aligned rows create a visible three-shell comparison gap.

teach_back

equal-balance-check

Teach Pip why 7 + 2 = 6 + 3 is true even though there is no answer box at the end.

Secure equality as sameness and prepare flexible missing-number reasoning.

Audio: Build both sides. If each side has the same amount, the equation is true.

Visual: Two sides of a balance each hold nine counters in different groups.

Question Variants

ma-y1-addition-and-subtraction-stories-q-birds-join

story-mat review difficulty 2

Six birds are on a fence. Three more birds arrive. How many birds are there now?

Three birds join the six birds, so there are nine birds altogether.

ma-y1-addition-and-subtraction-stories-q-cubes-left

picture-equation-match review difficulty 4

There are eleven cubes. Four are moved away. Which equation matches the story?

Four cubes leave the starting whole of eleven, so seven cubes remain: 11 - 4 = 7.

ma-y1-addition-and-subtraction-stories-q-missing-part

missing-number-balance review difficulty 5

Five red counters and some blue counters make twelve counters. How many blue counters are there?

Five and seven make twelve, so the missing number is seven.

ma-y1-addition-and-subtraction-stories-q-shell-difference

comparison-builder review difficulty 6

Mina has eight shells. Jo has five shells. How many more shells does Mina have?

After five shells are matched, Mina has three extra shells, so the difference is three.

Adaptive Support

low_sensory

Use plain story mats, muted object colours, optional sound effects and no confetti, flashing or hurried character reactions.

reduced_motion

Replace arriving, leaving and balancing animations with clear START, CHANGE and RESULT still panels.

audio_first

Speak every story, number and equation with replay; allow the child to answer by moving objects or speaking before reading symbols.

reading_support

Keep stories to one short sentence, pair text with objects or pictures and label symbols with plus, minus and equals audio.

attention_support

Show one story stage at a time, hide unused controls and use the predictable build-change-check-say routine.

processing_support

Allow unlimited thinking time, step-by-step object movement, undo, automatic alignment and repeated narration without penalty.

confidence_support

Preserve correctly placed objects, frame wrong answers as a story-model mismatch and avoid speed, streak or public-comparison pressure.

eal_support

Preteach join, arrive, leave, left, altogether, more, fewer, same and difference with gestures and matched picture stories.
