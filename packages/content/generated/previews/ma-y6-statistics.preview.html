Teaching Journey

warm_up

axis-scale-scan

The axis labels go 0, 20, 40, 60. What is each interval worth?

Retrieve scale reading before interpreting data points.

Audio: Before reading a graph, check the value of each interval.

Visual: Axis ticks pulse in jumps of twenty.

concept_launch

data-detective-lens

Graphs are clues. Check the title, labels, scale and units before you decide what they show.

Introduce a consistent read-check-interpret routine.

Audio: A data detective reads the whole graph, not just the biggest shape.

Visual: A checklist lens scans title, labels, scale and data.

worked_example

graph-point-trace

Use the line graph to find how much the temperature increased from 10:00 to 14:00.

Model reading two points and finding the difference using units.

Audio: Read both values from the scale, then subtract to find the increase.

Visual: Two graph points drop guide lines to labelled axes before a difference bar appears.

guided_practice

pie-sector-to-count

If 1/4 of 120 votes chose blue, how many votes is that?

Connect pie chart sectors to fractions of a total.

Audio: A sector is a part of the whole. Use the total to find the amount.

Visual: A quarter sector separates from a 120-vote circle and becomes 30 counters.

checkpoint

mean-stack-balance

Find the mean of 6, 8, 10 and 12 using fair sharing.

Diagnose whether the mean is understood as equal sharing, not just a procedure.

Audio: Add the values, then share the total equally between the four data points.

Visual: Four stacks level out to height nine.

teach_back

coach-scale-repair

Teach Nova why we must check the scale before saying the line went up by 3.

Repair scale-interval errors through explanation.

Audio: The line moved three intervals, but each interval might be worth more than one.

Visual: The companion counts intervals first, then reads the scale value.

Question Variants

ma-y6-statistics-q-line-difference

graph-reader review difficulty 5

A line graph shows temperature was 12 degrees C at 10:00 and 18 degrees C at 14:00. How much did the temperature increase?

The increase is 18 - 12 = 6 degrees C.

ma-y6-statistics-q-scale-interval

graph-reader review difficulty 7

A vertical axis is labelled 0, 25, 50, 75, 100. A point is halfway between 50 and 75. What value does it show?

Halfway between 50 and 75 is 50 + 12.5 = 62.5.

ma-y6-statistics-q-pie-quarter

pie-chart-builder review difficulty 5

A pie chart shows 1/4 of 120 votes were for blue. How many votes were for blue?

One quarter of 120 is 120 / 4 = 30, so 30 votes were for blue.

ma-y6-statistics-q-mean-fair-share

mean-balancer review difficulty 6

Find the mean of 6, 8, 10 and 12.

6 + 8 + 10 + 12 = 36. Sharing 36 equally between 4 values gives a mean of 9.

ma-y6-statistics-q-data-claim

data-detective review difficulty 6

A chart shows 18 pupils chose football, 12 chose dance and 10 chose swimming. Which claim is supported?

The data shows 18 for football and 12 for dance, so football had 6 more pupils.
