Adaptive Support

low_sensory

Use a plain mat, muted patterns, optional sound effects and no celebratory bursts.

reduced_motion

Use static before/after frames for exchanges, covers and balance changes.

audio_first

Speak every number, symbol and equation with replay and describe the model in reading order.

reading_support

Keep prompts to one short sentence; pair symbols with part-whole, frame or bar models.

attention_support

Show one relationship and one action at a time using a predictable build-hide-say-check routine.

processing_support

Allow unlimited thinking time, stepwise reveal, undo and replay of each transformation.

confidence_support

Preserve correct placements during repair and describe errors as a relationship to rebuild.

eal_support

Preteach part, whole, bond, related, equal, tens and derive with gestures and matched models.
